Research Areas

The primary focus of my research is the development of a non-invasive glucose monitor. The Glucosense uses a novel material to detect small changes in the glucose content in the blood through the skin. The cornerstone of the technology is a piece of glass that has been implanted with a thin fluorescent film. This has been made possible with a novel laser manufacturing capability, developed at the University of Leeds by Professor Gin Jose.
